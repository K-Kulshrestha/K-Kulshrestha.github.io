Hello! My name is Kshitij Kulshrestha and I am currently a third-year computer science student at The University of Texas at Dallas, I have a passion for technology. I'm constantly seeking opportunities to explore and acquire new skills in the tech industry. I have experience creating front-end interfaces as well as developing full-stack applications.

When I was 8 years old, I was introduced to the world of computers and I was fascinated by the way they worked. I was always curious about how the computer was able to do so many things and how it was able to process so much information. I remember watching my dad and my sister playing games together on the computer and I was amazed by the fact that they were able to play games on the computer.
