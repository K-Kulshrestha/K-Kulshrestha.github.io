Streaks

Progress : Ongoing

Problem

I felt like I was not being productive during my breaks. I wanted to create a habit of working out and reading. I felt as if I had lost connection with my friends and wanted to see what they were up to.

Solution

Streaks is a web application that allows users to create and track their habits. Users can create a habit and track their progress by checking off the days they have completed the habit. Users can also view their progress in a calendar view. Users can view their friend's habits and progress.
