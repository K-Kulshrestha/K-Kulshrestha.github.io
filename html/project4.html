Nurse Nav


Progress : Concept Finished 2022

Problem

We wanted to make healthcare more accessible to the public. Solve the problem of long wait times and the lack of information provided to patients. We wanted to directly connect nurses to patients to provide a more personalized experience.

Concept

Nurse Nav is a web application that allows patients to connect with nurses. Nurse Nav allows patients to view and search for nearby available nurses based on their needs. Patients can view the nurse's profile and schedule an appointment with them. Patients can also view their appointment history and leave reviews for the nurses.
