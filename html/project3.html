Policy Pioneer

Progress : Concept Completed Fall 2023

Problem

Small businesses have issues finding the right insurance plan for their company. There are many insurance plans available and it is difficult to find the right one. Small businesses also have issues comparing insurance plans. It is difficult to compare insurance plans based on their needs. Small businesses also have issues generating insurance quotes. Quotes are generated using many different parameters.

Solution

I came up with Policy Pioneer a web based dashboard that allows small businesses to view and compare insurance plans. Small businesses can view the different insurance plans and compare them based on their needs. Small businesses can also generate insurance quotes.
